POUNDED YAM WITH EGUSI SOUP

INGREDIENTS AND COOKING INSTRUCTIONS

POUNDED YAM

  • one medium yam
  • water
  • salt
COOKING INSTRUCTIONS
peel the yam, slice and wash and cook with water and salt till completely soft
using a mortar and pestle, pound the yam till soft and sticky, sprinkling the yam water at intervals
mould into desired shape and size and keep aside

EGUSI SOUP

INGREDIENTS FOR COOKING EGUSI SOUP

  • 5 tomatoes, 5 peppers and 1 big onion(Grated)
  • 1 tin Grinded Egusi
  • 1 serving spoon each of palm oil and veg. oil
  • 2 bunches spinach or ugwu washed and sliced
  • washed and chopped spring onion
  • 1/2 tin Grinded cray fish
  • 30 pcs sliced and cooked ponmo
  • 8 pcs seasoning cubes
  • 1 tsp curry
  • 1 tsp ginger and garlic paste
  • 1 tsp spoon
COOKING INSTRUCTIONS
Mix the egusi with water to form a paste
Add the oil into the pot and fry the grinded pepper mix for 5 mins, scoop out, add little oil and add the egusi mix gradually in ball
Fry for 3 mins, add the fried pepper mix and water, salt, ponmo and seasoning cubes
Allow to cook for 30 mins, add in the cray fish. Cook for 5 more mins then finally add ginger/garlic paste and sliced spinach and spring onion
Allow it to steam for 5 mins then it's ready to serve for lunch or dinner
